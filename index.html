Every design decision on this page was derived from the research database. Nothing was chosen because it looked good. The dark hero comes from dkb.emotion_design_matrix authority states. The amber accent comes from dkb.emotion_to_color. The marks are the actual marks library.

Design decision log — this page
#1A1410 hero bg
dkb.emotion_design_matrix: authority/mastery states use near-black, not pure black. HSL(30°,20%,9%) — warm charcoal signals permanence without coldness of developer-blue-black.
#C4622D accent
dkb.emotion_to_color WHERE emotional_state='authority'. Anti-pattern from database: blue overused by Linear/Stripe/IBM creating design-tool category confusion. Warm amber = earned knowledge, globally readable.
#F7F4EF body bg
dkb.color_research warm_background_reading_performance: 67.6% comprehension vs 56.5% cool/blue. Chosen over white (#FFFFFF) which dkb.emotion_design_matrix flags as clinical/sterile anti-pattern.
IBM Plex Sans body
dkb.font_alternatives: "Intelligent, human-centered, globally inclusive with enterprise authority." 54-language support for translation readiness. Carries institutional authority Inter's neutrality lacks.
SVG marks in hero
dkb.marks library, 14 of 29 marks rendered as actual SVG paths. Demonstrate the product — do not describe it. Every mark documented for emotional register in the database.
0px container radius
dkb.emotion_design_matrix: authority states use 0px radius. Containers signal permanence. 2px on interactive elements signals care without compromising authority signal.
