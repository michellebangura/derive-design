The process

Questions produce constraints.
Constraints produce values.

Most design decisions are made by whoever has the strongest opinion that day. Derive replaces opinion with a systematic question set — every answer constrains the specification, every specification cites its research basis.

01 — Ask
The complete question set

Who is this for, in their actual physiological state right now? What emotion do they arrive with? What must they leave with? What is the cognitive load budget? The time budget? The device reality? Every answer constrains what comes next.

→
02 — Research
1,991 indexed findings

Each answer queries the research database. What does the evidence say about this emotional state and color? This cognitive load and typography? This space type and acoustic treatment? The database has the answer. With citations.

→
03 — Specify
No ambiguity. Ever.

The output is not "warm and approachable." The output is: background #F7F4EF, IBM Plex Sans 300 at 1.7 line-height, border-radius 2px, animation cubic-bezier(0.22,1,0.36,1) 150ms. A builder who was never in the room implements it exactly.

"Design decisions without research are just opinions with authority. The authority doesn't make them right. The research does."

Design decision log — this page
Background #F7F4EF
dkb.color_research: warm backgrounds → 67.6% reading comprehension vs 56.5% on cool/blue. HSL 38°/22%/95% — warm enough to signal care, light enough to be open.
Accent #C4622D
dkb.emotion_to_color: authority + confidence states require warm, committed tones. Anti-pattern from database: blue accent overused by Linear, Stripe, IBM — creates design-tool category confusion for this brand.
IBM Plex Sans body
dkb.font_alternatives: "Intelligent, human-centered, globally inclusive with enterprise authority." Carries institutional weight Inter's neutrality lacks. 54-language support for translation readiness.
0px container radius
dkb.emotion_design_matrix: authority states use 0px border radius — containers signal permanence. 2px on interactive elements signals care without compromising precision signal.
Libre Franklin display
dkb.font_alternatives: "Classic American editorial, professional and versatile. Editorial heritage where Inter is deliberately neutral." Weight 800 for authority signal at display sizes.
